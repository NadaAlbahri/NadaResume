Founder and developer of 7erfa.om iOS mobile application.

The idea of 7erfa is that we try to preserve our Omani culture which has variety of traditional crafts that means a lot for Omani. It combines between e-shopping and spreading Omani culture. What makes us different from any e-shopping application, that we deal with most experienced craftsman. Also, user can learn and enjoy about Omani traditional music also he can view short videos about different crafts. What distinguishes us that our app was one of the best ideas in SAS program that done by the ministry of technology and communications in Oman. Now 7erfa is only available in Sultanate of Oman.
